# **App Name**: Canada Pathway Jobs

## Core Features:

- Job Listing & Details: Browse and view detailed information for available job positions, including qualifications, benefits, and deadlines.
- Multi-Step Application Form: A guided application process allowing users to submit personal details, educational qualifications, and select a job role. Includes fields for name, phone, email, ID, county, ward, desired job, and education level.
- M-Pesa Payment Confirmation: Submit the M-Pesa transaction code for the Ksh. 950 registration fee made to Till No: 4534716, as proof of payment during the application.
- Applicant Management Dashboard (Admin): A secure admin interface for recruiters to view and manage submitted applications, verify M-Pesa transaction codes, and track application status.
- AI Application Guidance Tool: An AI tool to provide tailored advice and suggestions to applicants on how to best articulate their qualifications or 'state the type of job' they are applying for, based on the job listings.
- Informational Content Pages: Dedicated pages detailing benefits of working in Canada, the application procedure (Steps 1-11), privacy policy, and contact information.

## Style Guidelines:

- The application will utilize a light color scheme to convey clarity and professionalism. The primary color will be a deep, trustworthy blue (#334D99), symbolizing reliability and depth, appropriate for official recruitment processes. The background will be a very light, almost white, cool blue-grey (#EFF1F7) to ensure content readability. The accent color will be a vibrant, clear blue (#2AA9DB), used for call-to-action buttons and interactive elements, adding a sense of energy and opportunity.
- For readability and a professional yet approachable feel, the app will use 'PT Sans' (humanist sans-serif) for headlines, providing a touch of warmth and modern appeal. The body text will use 'Inter' (grotesque sans-serif), chosen for its excellent readability and neutral, objective character, ideal for presenting detailed information.
- Use clear, professional, and easily recognizable icons for navigation and features. Consider specific icons to categorize job types or highlight application steps.
- Implement a clean, structured layout with ample white space to enhance readability. Application forms will be designed as multi-step processes with clear progress indicators, ensuring a smooth user experience. Key information like benefits and contact details will be prominently displayed.
- Incorporate subtle and tasteful animations for user feedback, such as form submission confirmations or smooth transitions between application steps, to provide a responsive and modern feel without distraction.
